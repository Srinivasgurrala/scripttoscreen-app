Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
ScriptToScreen runs on your PC. Your scripts and finished videos stay on your computer — we never receive them.

What stays on your device

The app is a desktop application that renders locally. Your scripts, generated voiceovers and finished videos are stored on your own computer. We do not collect, upload, or have access to them.

What the app connects to

To build a video, the app uses the free third-party accounts you connect:

  • An AI provider (for help turning your script into scenes) — your text is sent to that provider under your own account and their privacy terms.
  • A stock-footage library (e.g. Pexels) — to search and download clips.

These requests go directly from your computer to those services using your keys. We are not in the middle and do not see that traffic.

What we collect

  • Purchase & license: when you buy, our payment partners (Razorpay, Gumroad) process the transaction and share your email and order details with us so we can deliver and support your license.
  • Support: if you email us, we keep that correspondence to help you.

We do not sell your personal information.

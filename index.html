Questions

Good to know

Is there a money-back guarantee? ▾

Yes — a 3-calendar-day, no-questions-asked money-back guarantee. If it's not for you (or you get stuck in setup and we can't get you running), email us within 3 days for a full refund.

Can I see a real video it made? ▾

The clip at the top of this page is a real, unedited render straight from the app — footage, captions and voiceover, all automatic. Tap it for sound to hear the narration, and see the Examples section for more.

Is the setup technical? ▾

No technical knowledge needed. There's a simple one-time setup the first time you open the app (about 10 minutes) — the app walks you through it step by step, and it's free. After that, you just paste a script and go.

Do I need an internet connection? ▾

Yes — ScriptToScreen needs internet to fetch the stock footage and generate the voice & script. The video itself is then built and rendered on your own PC, and your scripts and finished videos are never uploaded to us.

Which operating system does it run on? ▾

ScriptToScreen is a Windows desktop app — Windows 10, 11 and later versions (64-bit). It installs in a couple of minutes. The first launch may show a Windows SmartScreen notice — click "More info → Run anyway"; the app runs entirely on your PC.

What are the PC requirements & how long does a render take? ▾

Any 64-bit Windows 10, 11 or later PC (~8 GB RAM recommended) — no special graphics card needed. Rendering uses your CPU, so it isn't instant: a 5-minute 1080p video takes roughly 20–40 minutes depending on your machine. Maximum video length is 12 minutes.

Can I pay with UPI? ▾

Yes. Indian buyers pay instantly via UPI (or card) through Razorpay; international buyers pay by card/PayPal. Your key is emailed within minutes either way.

Is it really a one-time purchase? ▾

Yes. You pay once and own the app — no subscription and no per-video charges, ever.

What voices and languages are available? ▾

Multiple natural-sounding English voices are built in. Need a specific voice or language (e.g. Hindi, Telugu)? Email us at animationany1@gmail.com — tell us what you need and we'll prioritise it.
